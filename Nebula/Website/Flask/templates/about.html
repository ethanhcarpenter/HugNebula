All About FTC

The FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a robotics competition that encourages students to design, build, and program robots to compete in exciting challenges each year.

FTC helps develop STEM skills such as engineering, coding, teamwork, and problem-solving. Our team, Cyberbyte UK 637, is proud to be part of this inspiring program and strive to push the boundaries of robotics innovation.

We participate in regional and national events, constantly improving our robot and sharing our passion for technology with the community.
